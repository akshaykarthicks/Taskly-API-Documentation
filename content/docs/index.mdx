## Welcome to Taskly.AI

**Taskly.AI** is an intelligent task management platform that revolutionizes how you organize and complete your work. Using artificial intelligence, it automatically breaks down complex projects into manageable subtasks, helping you stay productive and organized.

![Taskly.AI Dashboard](/images/dashboard.png)

## What is Taskly.AI?

Taskly.AI is a modern web application designed to help individuals and teams manage their tasks more effectively. Unlike traditional task managers, Taskly.AI uses AI to understand your projects and automatically create detailed action plans.
