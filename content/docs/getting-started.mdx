## Quick Start Guide

<Steps>

<Step>

### Create Your First Task

Click the "Create New Task" button and fill in:
- Task title
- Description (optional but recommended for AI subtasks)
- Priority level
- Status
- Due date

</Step>

<Step>

### Generate AI Subtasks

After entering your task details:
1. Click "Generate Subtasks" button
2. AI analyzes your task description
3. Receive intelligent subtask suggestions with timelines
4. Review and customize as needed

<Callout type="info">
The more detailed your task description, the better AI can break it down into actionable subtasks!
</Callout>

</Step>

<Step>

### Track Your Progress

- View all tasks in the main task list
- Click any task to see full details and subtasks
- Update status as you progress
- Check off subtasks as you complete them
- Monitor your dashboard for insights

</Step>

- See tasks due soon

- Track completion trends
- Analyze your productivity patterns
